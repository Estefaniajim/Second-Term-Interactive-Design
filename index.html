    Create your own methodology to develop a website. It’s important to justify all the steps

  • Make a demo on paper, including the things you need for your project, such as the need of your customer and how he wants the webpage to look like.
  • Start looking for the technologies you are gonna use, such as framework and the language you are gonna use. ex. Go, javascript, etc.
  • Make a real memo, with basic coding but just to show it to your customer and to make any early changes.
  • Gather all the information you are gonna use in your page, example, if it is a webpage about cakes, then get the information about the types of cakes, the shop and all the information that the customer wants in their page.
  • Start with the coding, use the framework and the language you choose in the previous steps.
  • Debug any issues you have with the code
  • After you finish, show it to the customer for finals changes, after this if the customer wants another change you should charged the customer a small amount.
  • Get paid or graded if it is for a school project.
